### About

B.Tech student at **IIT Kanpur**, working across embedded systems, vehicle dynamics, computer vision, and ML/signal processing — from custom PCBs and ESP32 firmware to the models and dashboards that make sense of the data they produce.

- Embedded systems & sensor fusion (ESP32, I2C, IMUs)
- Vehicle dynamics & ISO 4138 handling analysis
- Computer vision & complex-valued neural networks
- ML / signal processing for tabular and time-series data
